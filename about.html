Our Mission

To give every firm the authority to act with conviction

Capital decisions carry weight. The firms that make them deserve tools built to institutional standards — not retrofitted spreadsheets. VARKONIS exists to close that gap: to give boutique advisors, growing funds, and family offices the same analytical firepower that was once only available to the largest institutions on earth.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we build

01
Precision over speed

We never sacrifice accuracy for velocity. Every number on every dashboard has been verified, stress-tested, and sourced from authoritative data. Your decisions are only as good as your data.

02
Institutional standards for all

The tools that billion-dollar funds use shouldn't be out of reach for a 10-person advisory firm. We build enterprise-grade software that any firm can deploy from day one.

03
Transparency in everything

No hidden fees. No opaque algorithms. No lock-in. We explain our models, publish our SLA, and make it easy to export your data at any time.

04
Security as a foundation

SOC 2 Type II certified. All data encrypted at rest and in transit. Role-based access built in from the ground up — not bolted on.

05
Serve the operator, not the investor

We work for the people running the firm — analysts, CIOs, compliance officers — not the capital behind them. Our UX reflects that at every level.

06
Always be improving

We ship meaningful product updates every two weeks. Our changelog is public. Our roadmap is influenced by clients. We build with you, not just for you.

Our Story

From a spreadsheet frustration to a platform trusted by 840+ firms

2019
Founded in London

Two former quant analysts — frustrated by the state of mid-market portfolio tools — built the first version of VARKONIS in a serviced office in Shoreditch.

2020
First 50 clients

Word of mouth drove early growth. By end of year, 50 boutique advisory firms across the UK and Ireland were live on the platform.

2021
Series A — $8M raised

Raised $8M to expand the engineering team, launch the Risk Intelligence module, and begin expanding into Europe and Singapore.

2022
AI Insights module launched

Launched machine-learning-driven commentary and anomaly detection — the first platform to offer AI-generated client narrative at scale.

2023
500+ clients, $2B analyzed

Crossed 500 active firms and $2 billion in assets analyzed. Opened offices in Geneva and Singapore to serve European and APAC clients.

2024
Series B — $22M raised

Raised $22M to build out the Deal Flow CRM, enterprise compliance suite, and dedicated infrastructure for Scale-tier clients.

2025
840+ firms, $4.2B analyzed

Today VARKONIS serves 840+ firms across 18 countries, analyzes over $4.2 billion in assets, and processes millions of data points at 38ms average latency.
