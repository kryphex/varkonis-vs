Principles

We hire for people who can handle ambiguity without lowering the bar

That means sharp product instincts, comfort with technical detail, and discipline around execution. The work is demanding because the clients are demanding.

Clarity over theater

We prefer clear writing, strong product arguments, and measurable output over presentation-heavy noise.

Systems thinking

We build for interconnected workflows. Good decisions here require understanding the upstream and downstream effects.

High ownership

People closest to the problem are expected to drive it through, not escalate it endlessly.
